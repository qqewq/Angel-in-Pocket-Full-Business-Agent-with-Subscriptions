# GRA AgentOS Specification

## Generative Resonance Architecture (GRA)

GRA is a stability-first architecture for autonomous business agents. It ensures that generated business models, processes, and financial projections remain coherent and internally consistent across iterative refinement cycles.

### Core Components

1. **Stability Engine** – Analyzes business canvases for structural integrity and nullifies incoherent processes
2. **Multiverse Adapter** – Simulates multiple business scenarios in parallel to find optimal strategies
3. **Client** – Interface layer between GRA core and the Angel business agents

### Stability Metrics

- **Process Coherence** – Logical consistency of business process graphs
- **Financial Resonance** – Alignment between revenue models and cost structures
- **Strategic Harmony** – Compatibility of vision, product, and market positioning

### Nullification Protocol

When the stability engine detects incoherent processes, it applies nullification – removing or restructuring problematic nodes while preserving the overall business model integrity.
